Row School

The Results-Oriented Work School

About

We love learners. Whether we are going into schools to teach students CS or hosting them at a local, shared venue, Row School is designed as:
  • a learning environment that serves as an anchor of stability for the community and
  • a dynamic environment where learners are consistently supported and challenged.
We aim to help young students achieve a sense of purpose and meaning as they grow into adult learners.
