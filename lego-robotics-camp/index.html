Lego Robotics Camp

What is it?

We host a two-day weekend-long LEGO robotics camp designed to introduce students to engineering principles using the LEGO Education SPIKE® Prime™ set.

Students will learn about the components of the robot, how they work, and we'll talk about the social impacts of robotics in our world. Students will walk away from this course with a keen understanding of how sensors and gears work.

What do you teach?

Here's our lesson plan:

  • What is engineering?
  • Are humans like machines?
  • How do human sensors work?
  • How does a robot work?
  • Building a LEGO Education SPIKE® Prime™ car robot
  • Following a line, navigating a maze
  • Navigating a maze with multiple sensors
  • Power and speed with gears
  • Communicating a project or design
  • LEGO Robotics Jeopardy

How much is it?

$200/camp

When is it?

Two days (Saturday and Sunday), 10am-4pm
